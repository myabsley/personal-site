About Me, Shawn

I've always been drawn to tactile things. When I make jewellery, I go out into the world and look for components that make me reach out to them. I never know what the parts are going to become beforehand. I simply choose things that speak to me and trust that they'll eventually find a place in my designs. I love to make messy piles of metal chains, fibre chains, pendants, crystals and odd little bits until they "speak" to each other. That's how I know what will go together, and that's how my designs are born.
